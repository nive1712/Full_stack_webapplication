Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1: What are your bank's operating hours?

A1: Our branches operate from 9 AM to 5 PM, Monday to Friday.

Q2: How can I open a new account?

A2: You can open a new account online through our website or visit any of our branches.

Q3: What documents are required to open an account?

A3: You'll need a valid ID, proof of address, and your social security number.

Q4: Are there any fees for using ATMs?

A4: There are no fees for using our ATMs, but fees may apply for using out-of-network ATMs.

Q5: How can I apply for a loan?

A5: Loan applications can be submitted online easily, and loan payments can be made with one click. For any queries, contact our customer support.

Q6: What is the minimum balance requirement for a savings account?

A6: The minimum balance requirement is 500 rupees for a standard savings account.

Q7: How can I check my account balance?

A7: You can check your account balance through our online banking portal, mobile app, or by visiting an ATM.

Q8: How do I update my contact information?

A8: You can update your contact information through our online banking portal or by visiting any of our branches.
